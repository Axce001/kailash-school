About the School

Rooted in Vision,
Growing in Excellence

Formerly Dr. M.S. Udayamurthy School, Kailash School of Excellence was established in 2000 under the Kailasam Ponnammal Educational Trust. Inspired by the visionary ideals of Dr. M.S. Udayamurthy, we have spent over two decades nurturing confident, capable learners who embody the spirit of YES I CAN.

About Us

We are a passionate team of educators and mentors dedicated to creating a nurturing environment where every child can discover their unique potential. With attention to detail and commitment to excellence, we transform young minds into confident, capable individuals.

Academic Excellence

Structured CBSE curriculum with continuous assessment and exam-ready teaching methodology that ensures every student reaches their full potential.

Experienced Faculty

Qualified, student-friendly teachers who act as mentors and guide each child's unique learning journey with patience and expertise.

Holistic Development

Beyond academics — we nurture creativity, critical thinking, physical fitness, and moral values for all-round growth of every student.

Our Vision

Safe & Caring Environment

A nurturing atmosphere where every child feels safe, valued, and encouraged to ask questions and explore new ideas fearlessly.

Value-Based Education

Character building and moral education at the core — we teach honesty, respect, empathy, and responsibility alongside academics.

Future-Ready Skills

Digital literacy, scientific thinking, and life skills integrated into the curriculum to prepare students for tomorrow's world.
